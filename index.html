What is a palindrome?

A palindrome is a word, phrase, sentence, or number that can be read the same forward back backwards. (ignoring punctuation and capitalization)


About the palindrome finder

What does "palindrome" mean?

Palindrome comes from Greek palindromos, meaning "running back again," which itself is from palin ("back," "again") and dramein ("to run").
